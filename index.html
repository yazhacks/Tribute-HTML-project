Tribute to Technoblade

Technoblade: The Man, The Pig, The Legend

Technoblade was a popular Minecraft YouTuber and streamer known for his skillful gameplay, namely his incredible pvp skills, and entertaining content. Since the creation of his channel in 2013 and even after his passing, Technoblade has played a significant role in shaping the minecraft community and leaving a lasting impact on the game and its fans. Here are a few of the many accomplishments and stand-out moments from throughout his career as a Mineraft content creator:

  • In 2017, Technoblade beat Minecraft in hardcore mode using only a steering wheel rather than the traditional mouse and keyboard.
  • In 2018, on the Hypixel gamemode of bedwars, Technoblade managed to reach a winstreak of 1,868 before willingly ending it. This left the previous record of 374 consecutive wins in the dust.
  • In 2019, Technoblade participated in the Minecraft Monday events, where he faced many popular Minecraft players and won four of the events, his exceptional skills and strategic gameplay caused him to become a respected and even feared competitor.
  • After reaching one million subscribers, Technolade decided to forgo the face reveal usually done by faceless content creators after such a milestone. Instead, he hilariously decided to reveal an elbow instead to prove that audiences did not need to see a face to appreciate content.
  • The Potato War.
  • Technoblade won two Minecraft championships, consistently demonstarting his dominance in the competitive Minecraft scene.
  • Technoblade also won Minecraft Ultimate by a landslide despite his team being a player down. He broke both the individual kill record as well as the team kill record while raising a lot of money for charity.
  • Dream was one of Technoblade's biggest rivals and their interactions often sparked heavy debate regarding which of the two was the superior minecraft pvper. That's why famous youtuber Mr.Beast organzied a duel between the two for $100,000. Technoblade won the duel
  • Perhaps one of his greatest achievements was holding a charity livestream and raising about $350,000 for cancer research.
